External Quality Control

Besides being commercial textile, I also had the function of making the quality control of the garments before production shipments in order to check in advance quality problems.

We consider this service maximum importance and yes, it is an additional cost; true! But easily the clients get their money back when compared with a late discovery of failing quality. Being the goods already at your warehouse – or even already sent to shops or final client – it becomes much more complex and expensive to rectify.

Procedures
  • To know exactly which should be the final result / look of each article to be controled
  • To understand and to create a personalized and adapted file accordingly each client needs to make the full control
  • To go to each factory control and record all informations written and visual through pictures
  • To send to client all my records as well as our global feedback of control made
